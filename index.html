To deal with the sheer size of our dataset, we have randomly sampled 10% of the data in order to be able to handle it within D3 and properly display it through a web browser. To account for the reduction in size, we have multiplied applicable datapoints by 10 in attempt to keep visualizations as accurate as possible.
